Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

At Lune, we deeply respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or subscribe to our newsletter, we may collect the following data:

  • First and last name
  • Contact phone number and email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment details (processed securely through our payment partners)

2. How We Use Your Data

The information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send shipping status notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Send updates on new collections (only if you have opted in)

3. Data Protection

We implement advanced encryption technologies to secure your personal and payment details. We strictly adhere to privacy regulations and never sell, trade, or share your personal information with third-party companies for marketing purposes.

4. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, save items in your shopping cart, and analyze site traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences directly through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to request access to the personal data we hold about you, as well as to ask for its correction or complete deletion from our systems. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@lune-store.com.
